Our eco-pads provide for the predicted 1,000,000 girls who miss school due to periods in Northern Cameroon. In doing so we support them so they can learn and thrive at school and for the rest of their lives.

Conventional Pads

Conventional pads (most of which are imported from China) are either not available at all, or unaffordable for most women.

KujaEcoPads

KujaEcoPads are typically half the price of normal pads.

This affordability enables women to forget having to use unsterilised cloths (or whatever else they can find), which can cause pain, discomfort, shame and low self-esteem.
